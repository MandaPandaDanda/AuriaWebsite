Magic

Magic Type

Magic of the Golden Sun

Influence On Culture and Daily Life

Lumos magic is one of the most prevalent magics in Auria since it can be found almost everywhere since it is the magic of the sun. Royal light magic is the only form that is specific to Lumos with it being held by the current queen and princess, it is the power to hold and control sun rays as well as make those rays. Lumos as a kingdom does not focus too much on magic but it is seen throughout the kingdom.

Current Rulers

King Uriel Royal, Queen Nora Royal, Prince Rupert Royal, Prince Lucien Royal, and Prince Uri Royal, Crown Princess Lillyanna Royal

Culture

Art

Lumos' art focuses on stained glass murals made from either crystals imported from Musicorum or pur glass made by the glass masons all over the kingdom. The largest mural is in the center of the castle showing Eileen holding the tree of life.

Language Quirks

People from Lumos speak rather quickly and tend to speak more fancily than the other kingdoms, this is the reason Lillyanna talks the way she does.
