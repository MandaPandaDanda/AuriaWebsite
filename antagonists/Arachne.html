Backstory

Arachne was once the pride of her parents being a scentific prodigy that was until tragedy struck. Her parents and older sister were all killed when the Queen of Ignis destoryed their villiage in order to build an expansion for Ignis. This left Arachne to fend for herself, this where she learned how to make diffrent poisons from studying spiders. Selina found her when she was 13 and offered her a job as the Poison Master of Infurnum. From there she met Luan and went on missions with them while continuing to spread her kowledge of poisons further.
