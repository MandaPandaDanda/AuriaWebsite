Personality

Angel is very curious always wanting to explore the unknown, this led her to learning about Auria while in her realm. She is clever and uses her disadvantages as ways to make a fight turn her way.

Backstory

When Angel was born she was surrounded by loved from her mother and father to her older brother. The same day she was born the head dragon;s egg had hatched, this dragon being Aurora. Even though she can be called a "princess" her parents never treated her that way, instead allowing her to play with her brother and learn anything he learned. This is also where she learned how to fight and defend herself.

How They Unlocked Their Powers

Angel had unlocked her powers as soon as she was born when the dragon mark appeared on her. Ayra and Vasuki trained Angel and her brother together so they both learned how to fight and win. They were both taught to cover each others blindspots, Angel with her magic and Storm with dual blades.
