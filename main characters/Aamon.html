Personality

Aamon is closed off to many seeming cold and distant. But, once you actually get to know him he can be one of the sweetest people, in his own way. Aamon is smart and knows how to fight without using his magic since he is pretty scared of what Dark magic is capable of.

Backstory

Aamon was born to King Colton Vamp and Queen Kiara Vamp, his mother was always kind to him and helped him to not be scared of his magic. Growing up in Obscurum was not easy, the sun barely shone on the kingdom basking the people in darkness. Aamon was abused by his father since he wanted Aamon to unlock his magic to train him even harder. Kiara took Aamon to the kingdom of Glacies when he was around 5 and there he met Riku, the two boys became quick friends. Once Aamon had fully unlocked his magic, he was 10, his mother tried to protect him from his father but she was always pushed aside.

How They Unlocked Their Powers

All that Aamon can remember is that his parents were arguing and his father was about to hit Aamon's mom. Aamon's protective mind went into effect and he used his own magic on his mother to protect her.
