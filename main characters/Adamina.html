Personality

Adamina is feircly protective of the ruins of her kingdom. After what happened to Terra, she wants to get back at the Demon King for all the truama he had made her feel. She is kind to animals since her mother had always taught her to be acceping of everyone.

Backstory

When Adamina was born the kingdom ended up having a celebration because the Queen had struggled so much to have an heir to take over her place after she stepped down. She grew up with people always being there to help, even the animals helped her around the kingdom. Her mother began to teach her how to use their magic, she was 10, to continue to help the Kingdom grow in harmony with the forest around them. During Ada's 12th birthday celebration tragedy struck as the kingdom was attacked by monsters created from Demonic Energy that still existed in the Dark Wood. Adamina was wisked away from her mother by a spell and she was sent into the protection of the golums that protected Terra. After eveything calmed down the kingdom was in ruin, Adamina was left to fend for herself not even knowing that the other kingdoms could help.

How They Unlocked Their Powers

Her powers were unlocked with her mother's gentle hand as she helped Ada learn the Earth's powers through mediation and practicing the motions of using Earth's magic.
