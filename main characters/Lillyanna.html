  • Extra | Her left pupil is a star showing her royal linage

Personality

Lillyanna is someone who sticks with the royal code given to her when she was 8, but enjoys going against it from time to time. She wants to do what is best for her kingdom yet wants her own freedom. She wants to be free like her brothers. Lilly is an expert at building trinkets but she was also trained in battle tactics from her personal guard Alana Bright. She focuses on making sure no death is left in vain and that there are no extra losses.

Backstory

Born to the Kingdom of Lumos Lillyanna was always surronded by life and light. Queen Nora always made sure Lilly knew how much she was loved by her family and the people. When Lillyanna turned 5 she met Melody, princess of Musicourm, and the two were instant friends. When Lilly was 11 she had begun training with Alana to learn how to understand her importance to the kingdom and how to defend herself, this was where she unlocked her powers. At 13 she was crowned as crown princess, destined to take over the kingdom after her parents stepped down. This one event rocketed her into more extensive training to understand the royal code, she ran away from these lessons numerous times but was always caught.

How They Unlocked Their Powers

Lilly was training with Alana one day with her mother watching. they were using practice swords so Lillyanna would get used to holding a blade in her hand. Alana had gotten Lilly to trip on her own feet and quickly had to duck as a ball of light was shot directly at her face.
