Introduction To The Website

Do you like dramatic fantasy stories? Well, you have come to the right place! This webiste will help you better understand the story The Battle for Auria, which is a novel created by Manda Rumans and edited by Kyle Adkins, Julia Mcclintock, and Lainey Rivera. It is currently still in beta, but this website is degined to help new readers understand the main aspects of Auria.

The World of Auria has many twists and turns as more and more is found about about each kingdom and the corruption that is slowly taking over the land.
